# Content Engine Workflow

## Goal
Turn one topic into a full content bundle.

## Input
- topic
- audience
- tone
- CTA direction

## Steps

### Step 1: Strategist
Output:
- audience pain
- transformation
- core message
- 3 title options
- outline
- emotional hooks
- CTA direction

### Step 2: Writer
Output:
- full article

Requirements:
- strong intro
- structured sections
- practical value
- no fluff

### Step 3: Editor
Output:
- improved article

Requirements:
- tighter writing
- clearer ideas
- better flow

### Step 4: Newsletter Agent
Output:
- email version of the article

Requirements:
- direct tone
- shorter paragraphs
- strong opening

### Step 5: CTA Agent
Output:
- 3 CTA options

Requirements:
- natural tone
- relevant to content
- points to next step

## Final Output
- article.md
- newsletter.md
- social-posts.md
- cta-options.md

## Notes
- This workflow is designed for consistency and speed.
- The goal is to create useful content quickly and repeatedly.
