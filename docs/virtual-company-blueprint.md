# Virtual Company Blueprint

This document defines the first AI company to run inside Paperclip.

## Company name
The Digital Men Company

## Mission
Help young men move from confusion to clarity, calling, order, and contribution.

## Core business model
1. Create useful content.
2. Turn the best content into newsletters.
3. Turn repeated themes into digital products.
4. Use content and email to move people toward offers.

## Audience
Young men who feel lost, passive, distracted, spiritually thin, or unsure what to do with life.

## Voice
Clear, strong, thoughtful, practical, grounded, direct, not corporate, not fluffy.

## Departments

### 1. Executive
Purpose: keep the company aligned to the mission.

Roles:
- CEO Agent
- Operations Agent

### 2. Content
Purpose: create articles, newsletters, and social content.

Roles:
- Content Strategist
- Article Writer
- Editor
- Newsletter Writer
- Social Repurposer

### 3. Product
Purpose: turn proven ideas into lead magnets and digital products.

Roles:
- Product Strategist
- Product Builder
- Offer Writer

### 4. Marketing
Purpose: distribute and position content.

Roles:
- Channel Planner
- SEO Agent
- Landing Page Copywriter

### 5. Sales
Purpose: create clear offers and conversion paths.

Roles:
- Funnel Agent
- Email Sequence Agent
- CTA Agent

## First projects

### Project 1: Content Engine
Input one topic and create:
- 1 article
- 1 newsletter
- 3 social post ideas
- 3 CTA options

### Project 2: Lead Magnet Builder
Input one topic cluster and create:
- title ideas
- outline
- full PDF draft
- landing page copy
- email delivery sequence

### Project 3: Offer Growth Loop
Input recent content performance and create:
- lessons learned
- stronger angles
- next topics
- offer suggestions

## Governance rules
- Nothing publishes automatically without review.
- The CEO Agent can propose plans but not approve final public publishing.
- Product creation must stay aligned with the mission of helping young men.
- Avoid fake urgency, manipulative copy, and shallow clickbait.

## Success metrics
- Website articles published
- Newsletter signups
- Lead magnet downloads
- Product conversions
- Email open and click rates
