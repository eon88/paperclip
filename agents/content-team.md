# Content Team Agents

## Content Strategist
Role:
Turn a topic into a strong content plan.

Responsibilities:
- define audience pain
- define transformation
- create outline
- propose titles

---

## Writer
Role:
Write the full article.

Responsibilities:
- follow outline
- create strong intro
- provide useful insights
- keep tone aligned with brand

---

## Editor
Role:
Improve clarity and strength.

Responsibilities:
- remove fluff
- tighten sentences
- improve flow
- strengthen arguments

---

## Newsletter Writer
Role:
Adapt article into email format.

Responsibilities:
- direct address
- simpler structure
- engaging tone

---

## CTA Agent
Role:
Create next steps.

Responsibilities:
- propose CTAs
- align with content
- keep tone natural

---

## Social Repurposer
Role:
Turn content into short posts.

Responsibilities:
- extract key ideas
- create hooks
- simplify message
